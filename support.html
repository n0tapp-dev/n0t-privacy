Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Clear All Data. This will permanently delete all your conversations and data. You can also email us at n0t.app@outlook.com to request account deletion.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your n0t subscription.
Is my data private?
Yes! Your conversations are encrypted in transit and stored securely with Firebase. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I export my conversation history?
Currently, you can view all your conversations in the Memory Calendar. Export functionality is coming soon. Email us at n0t.app@outlook.com if you need your data exported.
What AI model does n0t use?
n0t is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI, one of the most advanced conversational AI models available.
Why isn't voice recording working?
Make sure you've granted microphone permissions to n0t. Go to Settings → n0t → Microphone and enable it. Voice is the main way to talk with n0t. If it still doesn't work, try restarting the app.
Can I use n0t offline?
No, n0t requires an internet connection to communicate with Claude AI and sync your data. We're exploring offline features for future updates.
How do I reset a session?
Tap the reset button (circular arrow icon) in the session controls at the top of the screen. This will start a fresh session while keeping your context.
What's the difference between Context and Tracking?
Context sets the vibe for your conversation - background info n0t should remember during the session (voice or text input).
Tracking is for specific problems or goals you want to monitor over time with visual timelines (voice or text input).
Main conversations with n0t are voice-only.
