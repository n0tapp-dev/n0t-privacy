Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Clear All Data. This will permanently delete all your conversations and data. You can also email us at n0t.app@outlook.com to request account deletion.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your n0t subscription.
Is my data private?
Yes! All your conversations are encrypted using AES-256 and stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your phone except when being sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I export my conversation history?
You can export conversations from the calendar view. Tap any date, then use the Export button to share your conversation history as text. You can also export all data from Settings.
What AI model does n0t use?
n0t is powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o, with text-to-speech and vision capabilities for voice conversations and AR features.
Why isn't voice recording working?
Make sure you've granted microphone permissions to n0t. Go to Settings → n0t → Microphone and enable it. Voice is the main way to talk with n0t. If it still doesn't work, try restarting the app.
Can I use n0t offline?
No, n0t requires an internet connection to communicate with OpenAI's GPT-4o API. Your conversations are stored locally, so you can browse your history offline.
What are Facts and Entities?
Facts are pieces of information n0t remembers about you (preferences, important dates, etc.).
Entities are people, places, and things you talk about. n0t automatically organizes them for you.
You can view and manage both from the Memory section.
How does AR Vision work?
Tap the camera icon, point your phone at anything, and ask a question. GPT-4o Vision analyzes what you're looking at and provides helpful information based on your personal context.
Can I upload documents?
Yes! Tap the document icon to upload PDFs, images, or other files. n0t will remember the content and you can ask questions about your documents anytime. Free tier: 5 documents, Premium: 50 documents.
