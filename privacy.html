Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 31, 2024

Introduction

n0t ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use n0t, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • User ID (generated automatically)
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)

Conversation Data

n0t stores your conversations and memories locally on your device:

  • Voice and text conversation transcripts and AI responses
  • Facts, entities, and memories you create
  • Uploaded documents (PDFs, images)
  • AR Vision scan history
  • Session timestamps and metadata

All conversation data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. Your data is never shared or sold to third parties.

Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Screen views and interactions
  • App performance metrics
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the n0t service
  • Remember your conversations and context
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send you important service updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Analyze app usage and performance

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • All conversation data is encrypted and stored locally on your device using AES-256 encryption
  • Data is protected when your device is locked (iOS FileProtection.complete)
  • Encryption keys are stored securely in your device's Keychain
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

AI Processing

n0t uses OpenAI's GPT-4o to process your conversations:

  • Your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for processing
  • OpenAI processes your data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not train AI models on your personal conversations
  • Voice responses use OpenAI's text-to-speech (TTS) service
  • AR Vision uses OpenAI's GPT-4o Vision for image analysis

Third-Party Services

n0t uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Auth: User authentication only (no data storage)
  • OpenAI GPT-4o: AI conversation processing, text-to-speech, and vision analysis
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your conversation history
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Conversation data: Stored locally on your device. Free tier: 30 days, Premium: 90 days
  • Older conversations are automatically deleted based on your subscription tier
  • Account data: Until you request deletion via the app or email
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

Children's Privacy

n0t is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us at n0t.app@outlook.com immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, by sending you a notification through the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: n0t.app@outlook.com
  • Support: n0tapp-dev.github.io/n0t-privacy/support
